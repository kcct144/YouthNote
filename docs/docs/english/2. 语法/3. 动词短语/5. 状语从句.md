## 状语从句

> 状语从句修饰动词（短语），表示时间、条件、原因等。由从属连词引导。
>
> **中考频率**：⭐⭐⭐ 高频。语法选择几乎每年 1-3 题考连词选择，短文填空常有 1 题连词填空，完形填空常考上下文的因果/转折/时间逻辑。

### 状语从句的九大类型

| 类型 | 常见连词 | 示例 |
|------|---------|------|
| 时间 | when, while, as, before, after, since, until | Call me **when** you arrive. |
| 条件 | if, unless, as long as | **If** it rains, I'll stay home. |
| 原因 | because, since, as | I left **because** I was tired. |
| 目的 | so that, in order that | He studies hard **so that** he can pass. |
| 结果 | so ... that, such ... that | He ran **so** fast **that** nobody caught him. |
| 让步 | although, though, even though | **Although** it rained, we went out. |
| 比较 | than, as ... as | She is taller **than** me. |
| 方式 | as, as if | Do **as** I told you. |
| 地点 | where, wherever | Sit **wherever** you like. |

### 高频考点

#### 1. when / while / as 的区别

| 连词 | 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| when | 某个时间点；"突然" | I was sleeping **when** the phone rang. |
| while | 时间段；两个动作同时进行 | **While** I was cooking, he was watching TV. |
| as | 一边……一边……；"随着" | **As** time goes by, we grow older. |

#### 2. if vs unless

| 连词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| if | 如果 | I'll go **if** you go. |
| unless | 除非 = if ... not | I won't go **unless** you go. |

#### 3. because / since / as 的区别

| 连词 | 语气 | 位置 |
|------|------|------|
| because | 最强，直接原因 | 常在后半句 |
| since | "既然" | 常在句首 |
| as | "由于" | 常在句首 |

#### 4. so ... that vs such ... that

| 结构 | 用法 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| so + 形/副 + that | 修饰形容词/副词 | He is **so** kind **that** everyone likes him. |
| such + 名短 + that | 修饰名词短语 | He is **such a** kind boy **that** everyone likes him. |

#### 5. although / though

> ❌ Although it rained, **but** we went out.
> ✅ Although it rained, we went out.
> ✅ It rained, but we went out.

**although 和 but 不能同时用！**（中文"虽然……但是……"，英文只用一个）

### 时态规则：主将从现

在**时间状语从句**和**条件状语从句**中：
- 主句用将来时（will do）
- 从句用一般现在时

| ✅ 正确 | ❌ 错误 |
|---------|--------|
| I'll call you when I **arrive**. | I'll call you when I **will arrive**. |
| If it **rains** tomorrow, I'll stay home. | If it **will rain** tomorrow, I'll stay home. |
