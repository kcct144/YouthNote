## 主谓一致

> 主语和谓语动词在**人称**和**数**上保持一致。
>
> **中考频率**：⭐⭐ 中高频。there be 就近原则几乎每年语法选择必考，the number of / a number of 是高频陷阱。

### 三大原则

| 原则 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| **语法一致** | 主语单数→动词单数；主语复数→动词复数 | He **likes** apple. / They **like** apple. |
| **意义一致** | 根据主语实际意义决定 | The police **are** coming. |
| **就近一致** | 动词与最近的主语一致 | Either you or he **is** wrong. |

---

### 高频考点分类

#### 1. 不定代词作主语

| 主语 | 谓语 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| everyone, someone, nobody, everything 等 | **单数** | Everyone **is** here. |
| both | **复数** | Both **are** correct. |
| all（指人） | **复数** | All **are** welcome. |
| all（指事物） | **单数** | All **is** well. |
| none | **单/复均可** | None of us **knows/know**. |

#### 2. 数量表达作主语

| 主语 | 谓语 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| a number of + 复数名词 | **复数** | A number of students **are** absent. |
| the number of + 复数名词 | **单数** | The number of students **is** 40. |
| many a + 单数名词 | **单数** | Many a student **likes** English. |
| more than one + 单数名词 | **单数** | More than one person **has** come. |
| 分数/百分数 + of + 名词 | 看名词 | Two thirds of the water **is** clean. |
| | | Two thirds of the students **are** here. |

#### 3. 并列主语

| 结构 | 谓语 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| A and B | **复数** | Tom and Jerry **are** friends. |
| A and B（指同一人/物） | **单数** | The writer and teacher **is** coming. |
| A or B / Either A or B | **就近** | Either you or he **is** wrong. |
| Neither A nor B | **就近** | Neither he nor I **am** right. |
| Not only A but also B | **就近** | Not only you but also he **is** invited. |
| A with/together with B | **与A一致** | Tom with his friends **is** coming. |
| A as well as B | **与A一致** | Tom as well as his friends **is** here. |

#### 4. 特殊名词作主语

| 名词 | 谓语 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| news, maths, physics | **单数** | -s 结尾但是单数 |
| people, police, cattle | **复数** | 集体名词 |
| family, team, class | **单或复** | 强调整体→单数；强调成员→复数 |
| glasses, trousers, shoes | **复数** | 成双成对的物品 |
| a pair of + 复数名词 | **单数** | A pair of shoes **is** enough. |
| the + 形容词 | **复数** | The old **are** taken care of. |

#### 5. 其他规则

| 主语 | 谓语 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 动名词/不定式短语 | **单数** | Reading books **is** fun. |
| 从句作主语 | **单数** | What he said **is** true. |
| there be 句型 | **就近** | There **is** a book and two pens. |
| 时间/金钱/距离（总量） | **单数** | Ten dollars **is** enough. |
