## 宾语从句

> I believe ==that we can win==.
>
> I know ==who you are==.
>
> I don't know ==what happened==.

宾语从句的构成：引导词 + 陈述语序的主谓结构

考点：

1. 引导词的选择（that, if/whether, 特殊疑问词）
2. 从句的时态（主过从过，主现从随意）

### 1. 引导词

| 引导词     | 含义             | 情景/例句                                               |
| ---------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| that       | 无               | 陈述，不缺信息，常省略 that<br>I think (that) he is OK. |
| if/whether | 是否             | 缺少或需要回答”是否“<br>I wonder if he is OK.           |
| what       | 东西             | I wonder waht you have.                                 |
| who        | 人               | I wonder who you are.                                   |
| where      | 地方             | I wonder where you are.                                 |
| when       | 时候             | I wonder when you'll come.                              |
| how        | 方式/感受        | I wonder how you came.                                  |
| why        | 原因             | I wonder why you came.                                   |

### 2. 语序

宾语从句的语序：陈述语序，先主后谓(SV)
- ✅ Nobody knows where you are.
- ❌ Nobody knows where are you.

### 3. 时态

1. 主过从过
    - She told me she won.
2. 主现从随意
    - I think she is playing games.
    - I think she was at home yesterday.
    - I think she will succeed.

### 4. 常接宾语从句的动词

1. “说”类：say, tell, report, explain, suggest, advice
2. ”想/认为”类：think, believe, suppose, guess, hope, feel
3. “知道/发现”类：know, understand, see, find, realize, notice
4. “希望/决定”类：wish, hope, decide, agree
