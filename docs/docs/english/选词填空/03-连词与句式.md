# 选词填空 · 连词与句式（专题 21–30）

> 从真题短文取空，每题 4 个选项（从该短文词框随机抽取）。
>
> - ==A 型（情况→词性）==：句子挖空 → 选正确单词
> - ==B 型（词→情况）==：给出单词 → 选它能填入的句子
>
> 💡 ==用法==：先看专题前的线索提示，再做题。每题根据语法线索判断答案的词性/意义。

## 第三课：连词与句式（专题 21–30）

### 专题 21：逻辑连词

> 线索：because / although / if / when / but → 填逻辑连词

#### A 型（情况 → 词性）

读句子，根据语法线索选择正确的单词。

1. She has realized the benefits of sports \_\_\_\_\_\_ is now a big fan of biking
    - A. seldom
    - B. city
    - C. grow
    - D. and

2. The heritage can't continue \_\_\_\_\_\_ there aren't enough people who are interested
    - A. break
    - B. always
    - C. spread
    - D. if

3. You can eat seafood at many restaurants \_\_\_\_\_\_ buy some fruit
    - A. another
    - B. fall
    - C. or
    - D. they

4. To keep the local fishing and hunting culture \_\_\_\_\_\_ to make sure it develops well
    - A. high
    - B. and
    - C. hole
    - D. seven

5. The lion ran away to save his life \_\_\_\_\_\_ the clever barber continued on his way
    - A. and
    - B. be
    - C. animal
    - D. his

6. I thought it was boring, \_\_\_\_\_\_ now I find it relaxing and meaningful
    - A. connect
    - B. but
    - C. develop
    - D. seldom

7. \_\_\_\_\_\_ students work together in the garden, they learn to communicate
    - A. When
    - B. seldom
    - C. encourage
    - D. join

#### B 型（词 → 情况）

读单词，判断它可以填入哪个句子的空白处。

    - B. \_\_\_\_\_\_ I went to our next-door neighbor Mr. Lee
