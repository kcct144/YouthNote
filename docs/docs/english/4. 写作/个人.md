## 话题分析

- 话题：个人成长与经历
- 占比：35%
- 子话题：
  - 【介绍】自我介绍
  - 【计划】梦想
  - 【记叙】我的一天

## 1. 自我介绍

### 写作框架

| 口诀         | 内容                               | 句型                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| 我是谁？     | 介绍自己的姓名、身份、家乡、性格等 | I am ...                                            |
| 我来自哪里？ | 介绍自己的特长、爱好、背景等       | I like ...<br>I am good at                          |
| 我要去哪里？ | 介绍自己的目标、计划、梦想等       | I am going to ...<br>I want to ...<br>I plan to ... |

### 我是

示例：

- I am Mike.
- I am a student.
- I am from China.
- I am outgoing.

### 我来自哪里

- I like reading.
- I play basketball every Saturday.
- I am good at speaking English.

### 我要去哪里

- I am going to help others.
- I want to be a teacher.
- I plan to travel the world.
- My dream is to be a doctor.

## 2. 我的梦想

| 口诀   | 内容 | 句型                                            |
| ------ | ---- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| 是什么 | 梦想 | My dream is to be a ...<br>I hope that ...      |
| 为什么 | 原因 | Because I want to help others.                  |
| 怎么做 | 计划 | I will ...<br>I am going to ...<br>I plan to... |

### 是什么

- My dream is to be a doctor.
- My dream is to open a restaurant.
- I hope that I can have a house in the countryside.

### 为什么

- Because I want to help others.
- Because I like delicious food.
- Because I enjoy planting plants.

### 怎么做

- I will keep studying hard.
- I am going to listen carefully in class.
- I plan to find a good job after graduation.

## 3. 我的一天

| 口诀 | 内容       | 句型                  |
| ---- | ---------- | --------------------- |
| 开端 | 时间、地点 | It was ... in ...     |
| 发展 | 谁+怎么了  | First, ... did sth.   |
| 高潮 | 谁+怎么了  | Then, ... did sth.    |
| 结局 | 谁+怎么了  | Finally, ... did sth. |

### 开端
- It was a rainy afternoon at school.

### 发展
- First, I was doing homework.

### 高潮
- Then, my teacher asked me to stand up.

### 结局
- Finally, he said "Happy birthday!"
