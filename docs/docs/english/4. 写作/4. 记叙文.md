## 记叙文模板

开头：Life is full of challenges and difficulties. I'll never forget the day when I ==did sth==.

- 困境：At first, I felt so ==feeling== that I was about to give up. 
- 突破：Then, it was ==somebody== who encouraged me to keep trying.
- 领悟：Gradually, ==my grades got better and better==, and I became more confident.


结尾：This experience have taught me a valuable lesson: ==sentence==.

## 示例：考试失利

Life is full of challenges and difficulties. I'll never forget the day when I ==fail the exam==.

At first, I felt so ==nervous== that I was about to give up. Then, it was my friend who encouraged me to keep trying. Gradually, my grades got better and better, and I became more confident.

This experience have taught me a valuable lesson: ==never give up==.


## 更多模板

### 1. 困境

- I had trouble ==doing sth==.
- I decided to ==do sth==.
- I still remember once I ==did sth==.

### 2. 突破
- With the help of ==somebody==, I overcame these difficulties.
- ==Somebody== carefully guided me to solve the problem.
- With a deep breath, I... 
- ==Somebody== told me, “...”


### 3. 领悟
- I realized that ==sentence==.
- Finally, I made it.
- To my surprise, I succeeded.
- I finally understand the meaning of ...
- This experience turned me into a ==adj== person.
