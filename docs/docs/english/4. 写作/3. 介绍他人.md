---
title: 介绍他人
permalink: /english/写作/介绍他人/
---

I would like to introduce my teacher to you. He is the most important person in my life.

- First of all, he is a teacher from China and he is very friendly. 
- What's more, he is interested in music and he listens to music everyday.
- In the end, when I have trouble in learning, he always helps me with my homework.

He is such a great person that I have learned a lot from him. I'll try my best to be like him.
